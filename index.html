About ASME SRIT

Our Vision

To be the premier student organization for mechanical engineering students at SRIT, fostering innovation, leadership, and professional development through cutting-edge programs and initiatives.

Our Mission

To provide SRIT students with opportunities for technical growth, networking, and hands-on experience through ASME programs, competitions, and events that bridge academic learning with real-world applications.

Recognition & Achievements

Best Student Section Award 2023

Recognized for outstanding programming, member engagement, and contribution to the mechanical engineering community through innovative events and workshops.

E-Fest Regional Champions 2022

Our team secured first place in the Human Powered Vehicle Challenge, showcasing innovative design and engineering excellence against competitors from across the region.

Community Impact Award 2022

Awarded for excellence in STEM education initiatives and outreach programs that inspired over 500 students from local schools to pursue engineering careers.

Innovation Excellence Award 2021

Recognized for developing sustainable engineering solutions and innovative projects that address real-world challenges in energy efficiency and mechanical design.
